Fortune Skies

Light up the night with explosive wins in Fortune Skies! Select fireworks to reveal cash amounts—match three to win a prize. More fiery awards are possible if you reveal a special firework to trigger a bonus game: spin the wheel in the Fortune Wheel Bonus to collect prizes or pick a firework in the Fortune Picker Bonus to multiply your wins!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Select eight out of twelve fireworks to reveal prize amounts or bonus symbols. Match three amounts to win a prize.

Reveal the Fortune Wheel Bonus symbol to trigger the Fortune Wheel Bonus. Spin the wheel to collect prizes. Land on the Arrow symbols to advance to the inner wheel for more spins. Land on COLLECT to end the bonus.

Reveal the Fortune Picker Bonus symbol to trigger the Fortune Picker Bonus. Pick one of three fireworks to reveal a multiplier, which is applied to all previous wins.

Any awarded bonus games trigger after all prize amounts in the base game are revealed.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.

In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable.
